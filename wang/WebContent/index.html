Wuxi Kwang Purification Equipment Co.

Wuxi Kwang Purification Equipment Co., Ltd is one of the leading supplier and manufacturer in purification equipment industry in China. Our factory is located in Wuxi city, only 50 minutes by train to Shanghai, which is very convenient for customers to visit us. With abundant strength, leading technology, excellent quality, perfect service, and penetrating insight of the market, we have developed a series of purification equipment. We have a strong & effective sales elite team and professional technicians and provide Perfect after-sale service. We offer air purifying equipment for microelectronics, biomedical, food industry, and pharmaceutical industry. Our main products: Air showers, Pass boxes, Clean benches, Rapid rolling doors, Sampling vehicles, FFU,HEPA Filters and other related products. We have rich experience in design and installation of clean room projects. We also accept customers' special specifications of the non-standard purification products.

Kwang Purification has always pursued the principle of "Quality is our culture". Our products have already set up good public reputation in the industry. We will do our best to create a safe, comfortable, clean and beautiful environment for you!
