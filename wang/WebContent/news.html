Air shower  >  Kw-AS-1 Single Person Air Shower


Kwang air shower's advantages and functions are as follows:

1)  Material: Stainless Steel SUS304;

2)  Electronic Interlock. When A door opens, B door is locking. A, B doors can not open when blowing;

3)  Emergency off button is installed according to customers' needs in case of emergency happens. Once press, two doors open;

4)  Color LCD screen, touch-point switch to control blowing time and lamp time. It shows reminder of HEPA filter replacement when needed;

5)  Infrared sensor automatic blowing, automatic stop;

6)  Two-side blowing, left and right, stainless steel nozzles. Three-side blowing can be provided according to customers' needs;

7)  Germany DORMA door closer, stable. China KFC stores also choose this type as their door closer;

8)  360-degree rotatable nozzles are installed according to customers' needs to keep high efficiency for blowing off particles from personnel;

9)  Have electronic interlock air shower patent.
