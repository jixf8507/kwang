Kw-AS Cold Steel Air Shower


Kwang Cold steel air shower is the equipment for clean workshop and small prefabricated clean room, used to blow the dust of the human body and cargo carried by the surface when they enter the clean room, as well as the cold steel air shower has the effect of air brake, to prevent the unclean air into the clean area, is the effective equipment for human body purification and to prevent the air pollute clean area.

Structure：

Kwang cold steel air shower adopt foam insulation color plate and aluminum alloy components to assemble, has centrifuge fan and primary & high efficiency air filter, is beautiful, compact structure, convenient maintenance, simple operation, etc.

Principle：

Cold steel air shower indoor air into static pressure tank by the fan through primary filter, after the HEPA filter, clean air from cold steel air shower nozzle come out at high-speed, nozzle is adjustable, can effectively remove the dust on the surface of personal or cargo, the dust will recycle into primary filter, work as a cycle.

Uses and features:

Cold steel air shower is the operator purification device used in clean room. Using high-speed clean air blow to get rid of the dirt on the operator. The nozzle can be adjusted, in order to effectively remove the dust in the people, and prevent the pollution into the clean area. It also can be applied to large cargo blowing.
