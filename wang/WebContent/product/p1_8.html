Air shower  >  Kw-AS Cargo Air Shower


Cargo air shower is blowing for cargo, also called goods air shower, is widely used in electronics, food, packaging enterprise logistics channel, to blow the raw materials and tools which will be operated & processed in clean workshop, to remove dust particles and guarantee the clean effect of the raw material of dust-free workshop. Due to the need of every enterprise logistics channel field size and blow pipe raw material size on the mode of transportation of goods is different, so the cargo air shower usually needs to be customized, We have general cargo air shower, infrared with voice prompt cargo air shower and automatic door cargo air shower. Kwang purification has designed many cargo air showers, has rich experience in construction, maintenance complete set of service, we will provide you with more comprehensive set of design scheme for your reference.
