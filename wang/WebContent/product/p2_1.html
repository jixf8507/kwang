Kw-CB-V1 Single Person Vertical Clean Bench


Kw-CB-V1 Single Person Vertical Clean Bench has the air inlet on its top.

The air is blown from top to bottom and can be filtered through primary, medium and high efficiency filtration. The product adopts the centrifugal fan with features of low noise and high air pressure. Its front windshield is the 5mm thick fully tempered glass. By using the plumb balance principle, the glass on the other side can achieve up and down movement freely and instant fixation for convenient operation. Meanwhile, the operational table is processed with novel punching.
