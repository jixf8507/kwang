Kw-CB-H1 Single Person Horizontal Clean Bench


Kw-CB-H1 Single Person Horizontal Clean Bench makes the fan set horizontally. The working noise of the product is lower than the vertical product. The product is produced with the outside dimension of 900×720×1420mm and the operational area dimension of 860×520×600mm. Three grades of air filtration can be conducted. For the air flow is horizontal, the product is often used for the electronic industry. The single person horizontal flow clean bench is equipped with one set of centrifugal fan featuring high air pressure and low working noise. The adopted high efficiency air filter allows the dimension of the plenum chamber to the lowest level. Then the size of the operational plate is much larger and the operational space is more spacious. The right and left sides of the product are 8mm thickened glass which makes the working area more spacious and brighter. Meanwhile, the high quality laminar flow equipment is applied for providing high clean environment.
